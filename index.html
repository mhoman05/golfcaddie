🔗 Connect to Notion

Takes about 3 minutes. No app login needed — you just copy two things from Notion's website.

Step 1 of 4
Get your Notion token

This is like a password that lets the app write to your Notion. You create it on Notion's website.

1
Open this link in your browser (sign in with Google as normal):
👉 notion.so/profile/integrations
2
Click the big + New integration button
3
Type any name — e.g. Golf Tracker — then click Save
4
You will see a long key starting with secret_ — click Show then copy the whole thing
It will look like this:
ntn_… or secret_…
Step 2 of 4
Create a database in Notion

This is where your golf rounds will be stored. Skip this if you already have a Notion database you want to use.

1
Open the Notion app or notion.so in your browser
2
Click + New page in the left sidebar
3
On the new page, type /database and choose Table — Full page
4
Give it a name like Golf Rounds
5
Now connect your integration: click the ⋯ button at the top right of the page → Connections → find Golf Tracker → click Connect
Step 3 of 4
Copy the Database ID

The Database ID is in the web address of your Notion database page.

1
Open your Golf Rounds database in a web browser (not the Notion app)
2
Look at the address bar — the URL looks like this:
notion.so/550e8400e29b41d4a716446655440000?v=abc...
↑ The highlighted part is your Database ID
3
Copy just that highlighted part — it is 32 characters long, only letters and numbers
Step 4 of 4
Sync your rounds
✅
Notion connected!
New rounds will auto-save to Notion.
❌
Connection failed
Check your token and Database ID are correct, and that you connected the integration to the database (Step 2, point 5).
